Raymond Williams

Coastal CaNEWS, Fall 2023

...Bob Whaley another person, I would not have known if not for this club. On one trip, I wondered why someone had a whale painted on a whitewater canoe until I met Bob and discovered his sense of humor. On my first full run of the Ocoee, I followed Bob’s line through several rapids because it looked like he knew what he was doing, and he did.

Years later, after a Goshen Pass run, Bob asked, “How many 80 year old World War II veterans have you run the Pass with?” I said, “As far as I know, just one.” When I asked if he was returning the next day, he said, “No I can’t do the Pass two days in a row anymore.” I thought that anyone still running Goshen Pass in their 80’s had a pretty good canoeing life.

Bob certainly did have a great paddling life and shaared that life with many. He will be missed.
